Societal Trade-offs

Introduction of AVs will involve trade-offs between safety, privacy, responsibility, and infrastructure control. Policymakers must balance these in ways that serve the public interest.

Ethical design choices must consider realistic road behavior, not just philosophical thought experiments. Ensuring transparency, fairness, and inclusive design will be critical to widespread adoption and societal benefit.
